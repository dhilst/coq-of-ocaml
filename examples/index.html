coq-of-ocaml Examples

Here we present some examples of the development version of coq-of-ocaml. Write at web [at] clarus [dot] me for more information. This work is currently made at Nomadic Labs in Paris. We use coq-of-ocaml to verify the implementation of the protocol of the crypto-currency Tezos, amounting to around 40.000 lines of OCaml. To work at Nomadic Labs, see our jobs page.
