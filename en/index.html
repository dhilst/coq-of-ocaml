Safe programming

Formally verify program implementations with the Coq proof system. Prevent bugs right from the start. For critical applications.

Developed at Nomadic Labs

We develop coq-of-ocaml at Nomadic Labs. This project initially started as part of a PhD at the university of Paris 7. We use coq-of-ocaml to formally verify the implementation of the crypto-currency Tezos. We are open to other applications for software verification.
